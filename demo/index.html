ABOUT Emerge

Decentralized , galvanized, Digital metaverse platform

Emerge is a collection of 10,000 unique spaces that make up a fully decentralized, massively multiplayer metaverse game where players own and can create limitless experiences & possibilities within each space.

The platform will also allow users to earn cryptocurrency rewards by playing the customized games or experiences created by builders inside the metaverse. And it goes all on the BSC blockchain.
